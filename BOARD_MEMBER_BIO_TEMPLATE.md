## ‚öñÔ∏è Keep it Educational, Not Political:

**Good Bio Language:**
- "Has voted in favor of curriculum changes including..."
- "Supported budget allocations for..."
- "Advocates for transparency in..."
- "Parents concerned about X may want to engage with..."

**Avoid:**
- "Always votes against parents"
- "Doesn't care about education"
- "Should be voted out"
- Political party references
