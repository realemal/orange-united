## Make Your Voice Heard

Orange United is a grassroots movement powered by concerned parents like you. Here are the many ways you can get involved and make a difference in Orange education.

## Ways to Participate

### Attend Events

Join us at school board meetings, community gatherings, and Orange United events. There's power in numbers, and your presence matters.

### Stay Informed

Knowledge is power. Subscribe to our newsletter, follow our social media, and read our articles to stay up-to-date on what's happening in Orange schools.

### Spread the Word

Share our content with other parents, neighbors, and community members. The more informed our community is, the stronger we become.

### Speak at Board Meetings

Your testimony at school board meetings can influence policy. We can help you prepare effective public comments and feel confident speaking up.

### Volunteer Your Time

We need help with:
- Attending and summarizing board meetings
- Social media management
- Event planning and coordination
- Graphic design and communications
- Community outreach

### Support Our Work

Running this organization takes resources. Your financial support helps us maintain our website, organize events, and expand our reach.

## First Steps

**New to advocacy?** No problem! Here's how to start:

1. **Subscribe to our newsletter** - Get weekly updates and action alerts
2. **Attend one school board meeting** - See how decisions are made
3. **Connect with us on social media** - Join the conversation
4. **Come to a community meeting** - Meet other concerned parents
5. **Share one article** - Help spread the word

## Skills We Need

Whatever your talents, we can use them:

- **Writers and editors** to help create content
- **Researchers** to dig into policies and documents
- **Graphic designers** for social media and materials
- **Event planners** to organize meetings and gatherings
- **Social media managers** to grow our online presence
- **Translators** to make content accessible in Spanish

## Time Commitment

Get involved at whatever level works for you:

- **Minimal (1-2 hours/month)**: Subscribe and share content
- **Light (3-5 hours/month)**: Attend events, occasional volunteering
- **Moderate (6-10 hours/month)**: Regular volunteering, committee participation
- **Heavy (10+ hours/month)**: Leadership roles, event coordination

## Questions?

Reach out through our contact form or send us a message on social media. We're happy to answer questions and help you find the right fit for your skills and availability.

**Remember:** Every voice matters. Every action counts. Together, we can ensure Orange students receive the excellent education they deserve.
