The Orange Unified School District holds regular board meetings on the first Tuesday of each month. All meetings are open to the public.

**How to Participate:**
- Attend in person at the District Office
- Watch the live stream on the district website
- Submit public comments in person or via email before the meeting

**This Month's Key Topics:**
- Budget review and allocation discussions
- Curriculum adoption votes
- Facilities updates
- Public comment period

**Public Comment Guidelines:**
- Sign up at the beginning of the meeting
- Speakers typically have 2-3 minutes
- Be respectful and stay on topic
- Focus on district business, not individual employees

We encourage all parents to attend at least one board meeting to see how decisions affecting your children are made.
