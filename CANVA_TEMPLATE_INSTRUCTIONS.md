## ğŸ“± Create Favicon (Browser Tab Icon)

### Step-by-Step

1. **Go to Canva**
   - Visit [canva.com](https://canva.com)
   - Sign up/log in (free account works!)

2. **Create Custom Size Design**
   - Click **"Create a design"** (top right)
   - Click **"Custom size"**
   - Enter: **512** x **512** pixels
   - Click **"Create new design"**

3. **Set Background**
   - Click **"Background"** in left sidebar
   - Choose a color:
     - Light Orange: `#FFF7ED` (recommended)
     - OR just leave white
     - OR solid orange: `#F97316`

4. **Upload Your Logo**
   - Click **"Uploads"** in left sidebar
   - Click **"Upload files"**
   - Select your Orange United logo image
   - Wait for upload

5. **Add Logo to Canvas**
   - Click on your uploaded logo
   - It appears on canvas
   - Resize to fill most of the square (about 400x400px)
   - Center it (Canva will show pink alignment lines)

6. **Download**
   - Click **"Share"** (top right)
   - Click **"Download"**
   - Format: **PNG**
   - Click **"Download"**

7. **Convert to .ICO**
   - Go to [favicon.io/favicon-converter](https://favicon.io/favicon-converter/)
   - Upload your PNG
   - Click **"Download"**
   - Unzip the downloaded file
   - Find `favicon.ico` inside
   - Copy to your project: `/public/favicon.ico`

**Done!** âœ…

## ğŸ“² Create OG Image (Social Media Share)

### Step-by-Step

1. **Go to Canva**
   - Visit [canva.com](https://canva.com)
   - Make sure you're logged in

2. **Create Custom Size Design**
   - Click **"Create a design"**
   - Click **"Custom size"**
   - Enter: **1200** x **630** pixels
   - Click **"Create new design"**

3. **Choose a Background Style**

   **Option A: Clean White (Recommended)**
   - Leave background white
   - Professional and clean

   **Option B: Light Orange Gradient**
   - Click **"Background"**
   - Search for "gradient"
   - Pick an orange/peach gradient
   - OR use solid color: `#FFF7ED`

   **Option C: Bold Orange**
   - Click **"Background"**
   - Choose solid color: `#F97316`
   - (If using this, make all text WHITE)

4. **Upload Your Logo**
   - Click **"Uploads"** in left sidebar
   - Upload your Orange United logo
   - Click on it to add to canvas

5. **Position Logo**
   - Place logo on the LEFT side
   - Size: about 300px wide
   - Position: centered vertically, some margin from left edge

6. **Add Title Text**
   - Click **"Text"** in left sidebar
   - Click **"Add a heading"**
   - Type: **ORANGE UNITED**
   - Position to the right of logo
   - Font suggestions:
     - Inter (similar to website)
     - Montserrat
     - Poppins
     - Or any bold, clean sans-serif
   - Size: **80-100 pt**
   - Color:
     - If white background: `#111827` (dark gray)
     - If orange background: `#FFFFFF` (white)
   - Weight: **Bold**

7. **Add Tagline**
   - Click **"Text"** â†’ **"Add a subheading"**
   - Type: **Protecting Parent Choice**
   - Position below "ORANGE UNITED"
   - Font: Same as title
   - Size: **40-50 pt**
   - Color: 
     - If white background: `#F97316` (orange)
     - If orange background: `#FFFFFF` (white)
   - Weight: **Medium** or **Semi-bold**

8. **Optional: Add Extra Text**
   - Add another text box
   - Type: **Parents and Schools. Together.**
   - Size: **28-32 pt**
   - Color: Gray `#6B7280` or match tagline
   - Position below tagline

9. **Final Adjustments**
   - Make sure everything is aligned
   - Leave margins (don't put text too close to edges)
   - Logo should be clear and visible
   - Text should be easy to read

10. **Download**
    - Click **"Share"** (top right)
    - Click **"Download"**
    - Format: **JPG** (smaller file size)
    - Quality: Leave on **Standard** or choose **High**
    - Click **"Download"**

11. **Optimize (Optional but Recommended)**
    - Go to [tinypng.com](https://tinypng.com)
    - Upload your downloaded JPG
    - Download the compressed version
    - This makes it load faster!

12. **Save to Project**
    - Rename file to: `og-default.jpg`
    - Move to: `/public/og-default.jpg`

**Done!** âœ…
